How it works

Three simple steps, one beautiful film

You don't need to be good with computers, and you don't need perfect photos. You send what you have — we do the rest, by hand.

01

Send your photos

Up to 120 photos and little video clips — snapshots, old prints, phone pictures. Tell us who they're for and the feeling you want.

02

We craft the film

We gently restore and colour the photos, add warm narration, music and cinematic motion, and shape it into a story that flows.

03

Yours in 48–72 hours

A polished three-to-five-minute film arrives ready to play at a service, share with family, or keep forever. One free tweak included.

04

Tenderly made

Never a template churned out by a machine. Every film is finished by a real person who treats it like their own family's.

05

Ready for the day

Delivered in the right format for a funeral screen, a phone, or social media — with a downloadable copy that's yours to keep.

06

Handled with care

Your photos stay private, are never shared, and are deleted after delivery unless you'd like us to keep them for a future film.
